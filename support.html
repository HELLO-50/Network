Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a course?

You can sign up for a course by visiting our Courses page, selecting the course you're interested in, and clicking the "Sign Up" button.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can find more details on the payment options during the checkout process.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing us at educationgateway44@gmail.com or by using the contact form on our Contact Us page.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with a course, you can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
